Optimization

main application domain of EAs. Optimization is the process of determining or approximating object parameter sets that maximize resp. minimize a given objective function(s). A distinction is made between global and local optimization (local search). Optimal states are always defined with respect to a given neighborhood in the search space. For global optima the neighborhood includes the whole search space. Depending on the structure of the search space parameter, combinatorial, or structure optimization are distinguished.
